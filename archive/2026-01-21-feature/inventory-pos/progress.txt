### Completed Stories

#### US-109 - Inventory schema ✅
- **Completed**: 2026-01-21
- **Summary**: Verified all database models for product inventory management are in place
- **Models Implemented**:
  - ProductCategory model with name, description, slug, sortOrder, hierarchical parent/child
  - Product model with name, sku, categoryId, description, retailPrice, costPrice, isTaxable, barcode, weight, dimensions, inventory settings
  - InventoryItem model with productId, quantity, location, reservedQty, availableQty, averageCost
  - InventoryMovement model for tracking quantity changes (serves as InventoryAdjustment)
  - Vendor model with name, contactName, email, phone, address, paymentTerms
  - PurchaseOrder model with vendorId, items, status, totals, shipping info
  - VendorProduct model for vendor-specific product info
  - Sale and SaleItem models for POS transactions
  - LowStockAlert model for inventory alerts
- **Verification**: Prisma generate succeeds, TypeScript compiles without errors

#### US-110 - Product catalog management ✅
- **Completed**: 2026-01-21

- **Procedures Implemented**:
