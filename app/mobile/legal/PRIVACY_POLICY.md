# ChiroFlow Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

ChiroFlow Inc. ("ChiroFlow," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App") and related services.

By using ChiroFlow, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Name, email address, phone number
- Professional credentials (for providers)
- Organization/practice information

**Health Information**
- Patient demographic information
- Clinical documentation and notes
- Treatment plans and progress
- Appointment history
- Progress photos

**Communications**
- Messages between providers and patients
- Support inquiries
- Feedback and reviews

### Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information**
- Device type, operating system, version
- Unique device identifiers
- Push notification tokens

**Usage Information**
- Features accessed and time spent
- Interaction patterns
- Error logs and crash reports

**Location Information (Optional)**
- Approximate location for check-in verification
- Only collected with explicit permission

## How We Use Your Information

### To Provide Services
- Manage your account and authentication
- Enable scheduling and appointment management
- Store and sync clinical documentation
- Deliver push notifications
- Enable offline functionality

### To Improve Services
- Analyze usage patterns
- Fix bugs and errors
- Develop new features
- Optimize performance

### To Communicate
- Send appointment reminders
- Provide support
- Share important updates
- Respond to inquiries

### For Security
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Maintain audit logs
- Comply with legal requirements

## Data Sharing

### We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share health information for advertising
- Use your data for purposes unrelated to ChiroFlow services

### We May Share With:

**Service Providers**
Third parties that help us operate our services (hosting, analytics, push notifications), bound by confidentiality agreements.

**Practice Organizations**
Information is shared within your associated practice/organization as necessary for care coordination.

**Legal Requirements**
When required by law, subpoena, or legal process.

**Business Transfers**
In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

## HIPAA Compliance

ChiroFlow is designed to help covered entities comply with HIPAA:

- **Business Associate Agreement**: We enter into BAAs with healthcare providers
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access to protected health information
- **Encryption**: All PHI encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Audit Logs**: Comprehensive logging of all PHI access
- **Breach Notification**: Procedures in place for security incidents

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption**: TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
- **Authentication**: Multi-factor authentication support
- **Biometric**: Face ID/Touch ID/Fingerprint authentication
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring
- **Testing**: Regular security assessments and penetration testing

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Clinical Records**: Retained per applicable healthcare regulations (typically 7-10 years)
- **Account Deletion**: Upon request, we delete data within 30 days, except as required by law
- **Audit Logs**: Retained for 6 years per HIPAA requirements

## Children's Privacy

ChiroFlow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

- **Know**: What personal information is collected
- **Delete**: Request deletion of personal information
- **Opt-Out**: We do not sell personal information
- **Non-Discrimination**: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

## International Users

ChiroFlow is operated from the United States. If you access our services from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the US, which may have different data protection laws.

## Push Notifications

We send push notifications for:
- Appointment reminders
- Schedule changes
- Messages from your care team

## Offline Data
