## Description (4000 chars max)

```
ChiroFlow is the comprehensive practice management solution designed specifically for chiropractic clinics. Streamline your daily operations, improve patient care, and grow your practice with our all-in-one mobile app.

FOR PROVIDERS:
• View and manage your daily schedule at a glance
• Quick patient check-in with one tap
• Document encounters with voice-to-text SOAP notes
• Capture clinical photos and annotate body diagrams
• Access patient history and treatment plans anywhere
• Receive real-time schedule change notifications
• Work offline and sync when connected

FOR PATIENTS:
• Book, reschedule, or cancel appointments easily
• Check in when you arrive at the clinic
• View your upcoming appointments and visit history
• Access home exercise videos and instructions
• Track your pain levels and progress
• Communicate securely with your care team
• Get appointment reminders and health tips

KEY FEATURES:
✓ Mobile-Optimized Charting - Document care quickly with templates, voice dictation, and touch-based body diagrams
✓ Intelligent Scheduling - View availability, manage blocks, and handle walk-ins efficiently
✓ Offline Capabilities - Keep working even without internet connection
✓ Secure Messaging - HIPAA-compliant communication between staff and patients
✓ Progress Tracking - Visual progress photos and pain diary for better outcomes
✓ Push Notifications - Never miss an appointment or important update

DESIGNED FOR CHIROPRACTORS:
ChiroFlow understands chiropractic workflows. Our mobile app includes specialized features like subluxation listings, adjustment tracking, spinal analysis tools, and technique-specific documentation that generic EHR systems simply don't offer.

HIPAA COMPLIANT & SECURE:
Your patient data is protected with enterprise-grade security including end-to-end encryption, biometric authentication, and comprehensive audit logging. We take privacy seriously.

SEAMLESS INTEGRATION:
ChiroFlow mobile works seamlessly with the ChiroFlow web platform. Your data syncs automatically, so you can start a note on your phone and finish it on your desktop.

SUBSCRIPTION REQUIRED:
ChiroFlow mobile requires an active ChiroFlow practice subscription. Visit chiroflow.com to learn more about our plans and start your free trial.

SUPPORT:
Questions? Our support team is here to help.
• Email: support@chiroflow.com
• Help Center: help.chiroflow.com
• In-app: Settings > Help & Support
```

### Notes for App Review
```
ChiroFlow is a healthcare practice management application for chiropractic clinics.

Key points for review:
1. HIPAA Compliance: This app handles protected health information (PHI) and implements HIPAA-compliant security measures including encryption and access controls.

2. Authentication: Users must have an active ChiroFlow subscription and valid credentials to access the app. A demo account is provided above for testing purposes.

3. Medical Disclaimer: This app is for practice management purposes only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.

4. Offline Mode: The app caches data for offline access. Test by enabling airplane mode after initial login.

5. Push Notifications: The app uses push notifications for appointment reminders and messages. Notifications are user-configurable.

6. Camera/Photo Access: Used for documenting patient progress photos and attaching images to clinical notes. All photos are stored securely and encrypted.

7. Location Access: Optional feature for patient check-in proximity verification.

8. Microphone Access: Used for voice-to-text documentation feature.

Please contact appstore@chiroflow.com if you have any questions during the review process.
```
