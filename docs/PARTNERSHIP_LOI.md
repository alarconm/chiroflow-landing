## 1. Purpose

This Letter of Intent ("LOI") outlines the mutual understanding between ChiroFlow and Partner regarding a development partnership to create AI-powered automation solutions for chiropractic practices.

This is a non-binding expression of intent, except for Sections 6 (Confidentiality) and 7 (Exclusivity), which are binding.

---

## 2. Partnership Overview

### 2.1 What ChiroFlow Will Provide

- [ ] AI automation platform development at no cost to Partner during development phase
- [ ] Technical implementation and integration with Partner's existing systems
- [ ] Ongoing support and optimization
- [ ] HIPAA-compliant infrastructure and security measures
- [ ] Regular progress updates and collaboration sessions

### 2.2 What Partner Will Provide

- [ ] Access to existing Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and documentation
- [ ] Test environment access to practice management systems
- [ ] Dedicated time for discovery sessions (estimated: 4-6 hours initially)
- [ ] Feedback on automation performance and accuracy
- [ ] Introductions to other practice owners (optional, after success demonstrated)

## 6. Confidentiality (BINDING)

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of:

**ChiroFlow agrees not to disclose:**
- Partner's patient information (PHI)
- Partner's financial data
- Partner's proprietary business processes
- Any information marked confidential by Partner

**Partner agrees not to disclose:**
- ChiroFlow's technology and architecture
- ChiroFlow's business plans and pricing
- ChiroFlow's other customer information
- Any information marked confidential by ChiroFlow

**Duration:** This confidentiality obligation survives for 3 years after termination of this partnership.

**Exceptions:** Information that is:
- Publicly available through no fault of receiving party
- Independently developed without use of confidential information
- Required to be disclosed by law (with notice to other party)

---

## 7. Exclusivity (BINDING)

During the development phase (estimated 90 days from signing):

- **ChiroFlow** will not engage another chiropractic practice as a development partner in the same geographic market
- **Partner** will not engage another AI automation vendor for similar services

After the development phase, this exclusivity terminates automatically.

---

## 8. HIPAA Compliance

ChiroFlow agrees to:
- [ ] Execute a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) before accessing any PHI
- [ ] Implement appropriate administrative, physical, and technical safeguards
- [ ] Report any security incidents within 24 hours
- [ ] Allow Partner to audit compliance upon reasonable request

---

## 9. Intellectual Property

- **Partner's SOPs**: Partner retains ownership of original documentation
- **ChiroFlow's Technology**: ChiroFlow retains ownership of all software, algorithms, and AI systems
- **Derived Works**: Automation workflows created from Partner's SOPs become jointly owned, with ChiroFlow having exclusive license to commercialize

---

## 10. Termination

Either party may terminate this LOI with 30 days written notice if:
- Material breach by the other party that remains uncured for 14 days
- Mutual agreement to discontinue
- Failure to achieve success metrics after good faith effort (90+ days)

Upon termination:
- Partner retains any equity vested to date
- ChiroFlow returns or destroys all Partner confidential information
- Partner may continue using automations deployed (if any), subject to commercial terms
