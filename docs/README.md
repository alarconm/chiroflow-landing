## Meeting Agenda: First Call with Kent

1. **Intro (5 min)** - Relationship, why we're excited
2. **Discovery Questionnaire (45 min)** - Walk through together
3. **Technical Overview (15 min)** - What systems he uses
4. **Partnership Options (15 min)** - Present structures, gauge interest
5. **Next Steps (10 min)** - SOP handoff, schedule follow-ups

## What to Send Kent Before Meeting

1. Link to landing page: https://chiroflow-landing-production.up.railway.app/
2. ROI Calculator (or just show him live)
3. Brief overview of what we're proposing (1-pager)

## After the Meeting

1. Send thank you + summary email
2. Share secure folder for SOP upload
3. Schedule technical deep-dive
4. Prepare LOI with chosen partnership option
